AWARENESS

“The ACS acts as a platform to promote the African and Caribbean lifestyle, creating awareness for our struggles, our triumphs, our culture and our future.”
-Folarin Osibajo, class ’19, Nigeria.
-------

The ACS provides a platform to create awareness in different ways on different matters. We strive to continuously enlighten members as well as all others interested, about common misconceptions and issues faced by the African and Caribbean societies. Also, we members form as supportive structure to ease the academic stress on one another. Our aim is to have an educated membership and student body as a whole.

CULTURE

“The society helped me appreciate culture by organising a range of events from quiz nights to fashion shows, each exploring different African and Caribbean cultures.”
-Maame Abena, class ’19, Ghana.
-------

The society’s membership mainly comprises of persons with African or Caribbean heritage hence its values the display and preservation of such cultures. The ACS provides an avenue for the expression and celebration of cultural beliefs of the members. This is manifested in most of its events which more often than not, serve as a cultural showcase.

SUPPORT

“Tthe ACS has provided a warm place for me to celebrate the culture of Africa and the diaspora, and gives us the ability to support each other throughout our educational journey at Durham.”
-Ryan Robinson, class ’19, Bermuda.
-------

Tthe ACS community goes beyond being a group of members, we seek to form a close bond amongst members which goes on to serve as a strong platform and support structure upon which members can find stability in the college living.
